ABOUT ME

I am the main antagonist in the Japanese animated film, Spirited Away.


I am greedy for wealth and it has a higher priority over my family. I have a younger twin sister named Zeniba, but I am the prettier one. I also have a giant-sized baby named, Boh.


I have magical abilities that allow me to "steal" the names of the workers and binds them to a contract forever, unless they remember their stolen name. I may be selfish and frugal, but I display motherly qualities for taking care of Boh.
